# Analytics & Insights

## Personal Analytics Dashboard

### Workout Analytics
- Workouts per week/month trend chart
- Workout duration tracking and trends
- Exercise volume progression (weight x reps over time)
- Muscle group frequency heatmap
- Personal records (PRs) tracking and history
- Workout consistency score

### Macro Analytics
- Calorie intake trends (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Macro split visualization (pie chart, stacked bar)
- Protein per pound of bodyweight tracking
- Adherence percentage over time
- Most logged foods/meals
- Nutrition consistency score

### Progress Analytics
- Weight trend with moving average
- Body measurements tracking (chest, waist, arms, etc.)
- Progress photos with side-by-side comparison
- Estimated body composition (if measurements provided)
- Goal progress visualization

### XP & Gamification Analytics
- XP earned per week breakdown (by source)
- Level-up velocity (time between levels)
- Streak history and patterns
- Badge collection progress
- Predicted time to next rank

---

## Insights & Recommendations

### Pattern Detection
- "You tend to miss workouts on Wednesdays"
- "Your protein is lower on weekends"
- "You've been consistent for 3 weeks straight"

### Smart Recommendations
- Suggested workout days based on history
- Macro adjustments based on progress
- Rest day recommendations based on volume

### Milestone Celebrations
- "You've logged 100 workouts!"
- "1 year anniversary on Trained"
- "Personal best streak achieved"

## Reporting

### Weekly Summary
- Email digest option (opt-in)
- In-app weekly recap modal
- Key stats: workouts, adherence, XP earned, streak status

### Monthly Report
- Comprehensive monthly review
- Progress vs previous month
- Goal check-in and adjustment suggestions
- Shareable summary image for social

### Export Options
- CSV export of all data
- PDF report generation
- Apple Health / Google Fit export

## Coach Analytics (Coach Dashboard)

### Client Overview
- Client activity heatmap (who's active, who's not)
- Compliance rates across all clients
- At-risk clients (dropping off)

### Individual Client Deep Dive
- Full workout history with notes
- Macro adherence patterns
- Weight trend analysis
- Check-in history and responses

### Aggregate Analytics
- Average client retention
- Most effective workout programs
- Common client struggles
